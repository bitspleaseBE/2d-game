# Story of Wandertrap

Every night, Theo climbs into bed in his blue pyjamas with one promise to himself: tonight, no monsters, no mazes, just sleep.

But as moonlight fills his room, a golden dream-shard blinks beneath his pillow and opens a doorway into the Wandertrap. Inside the dream-labyrinth, Sleep Thief orcs have stolen the shards that show the way back to morning, hiding them behind gates, traps, and stone walls.

Their Wardens guard the deeper halls, and far below them the Orc King gathers the last shard on a throne made from broken nightmares.

Theo tightens his pyjama sleeves, lifts his sword, and steps forward. Reclaim the shards. Defeat the Orc King. Wake before dawn.

When the last shard is restored, the Wandertrap fades into sunrise and Theo wakes safe beneath his blanket, still brave in his blue pyjamas.
